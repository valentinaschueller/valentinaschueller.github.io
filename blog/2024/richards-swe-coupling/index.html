Coupled groundwater-surface flows

  • 01 March 2024
  • research,
  • english

I aim to have one short summary post for each of my active/finished research projects. This is one of them.

In this project, we analyze coupled surface-subsurface flows for hydrological applications. These are modeled using nonlinear partial differential equations: Richards' equation for groundwater flow and a variant of the shallow water equations for surface flow. Coupling them yields a model that can simulate effects of water management and flooding on the water table and aquifer.[1]

Particularly, we have analyzed a linearized, coupled Richards-SWE model using methods developed by Monge & Birken, 2018 and Gander, Kwok & Mandal, 2014. We study how well this analysis works for the fully nonlinear case: For this purpose, I have developed a Python implementation for coupled surface-subsurface flow, using DUNE to generate the discretization and preCICE for coupling.
